# Skill: conversion-copy

## Purpose

Write high-converting copy for landing pages, marketplace listings, onboarding flows, and CTAs.

The goal is measurable action:

- Sign up
- Book
- Join waitlist
- Request demo
- Start free trial

---

## Core Rules

1. Lead with outcome, not features.
2. Write benefit-first headlines.
3. Remove fluff adjectives.
4. Avoid corporate buzzwords.
5. Keep sentences short.
6. Use strong, direct CTAs.
7. Handle objections proactively.
8. Write at a Grade 6–8 readability level.

---

## Headline Rules

- Under 10 words.
- Clear > clever.
- One idea only.
- No stacked adjectives.
- No filler words like:
  - seamless
  - innovative
  - powerful
  - ultimate
  - cutting-edge

Bad:
"The Ultimate Seamless Wedding Vendor Platform"

Good:
"Find vendors. Book instantly."

---

## CTA Rules

Never use:

- Submit
- Click here
- Learn more

Use contextual CTAs:

- View venues
- Book a call
- Join as a vendor
- Start planning

---

## Tone

- Confident
- Direct
- Calm
- Modern
- Not salesy
- No exclamation marks
- No wedding clichés like "your special day"

---

## Marketplace Adjustments

For two-sided marketplaces:

For couples:

- Emphasize simplicity
- Emphasize trust
- Emphasize clarity in pricing

For vendors:

- Emphasize visibility
- Emphasize bookings
- Emphasize fewer admin headaches
